# Moonshots EP 253: Demis Hassabis on AGI, Robotics & The Future
**Source:** Peter Diamandis, Moonshots podcast EP 253 (with Demis Hassabis, DeepMind/Google)
**Duration:** ~94 minutes  
**Date:** April 2026

## Key Takeaways

### 1. AGI and AI Development
- Current LLMs may already be on the path toward AGI, or entirely new breakthroughs are needed
- AI's role in accelerating scientific discovery is a central theme
- The discussion examines what AGI actually means in practice

### 2. Robotics and Future Production
- Humanoid robots in manufacturing: scaling implications
- Economic and labor market transformation ahead

### 3. Elon Musk's Moonshot Governance Model
- Massive CEO compensation packages (Tesla example) as a new model for funding ambitious projects
- Mars colonization at $1 trillion cost — the math, timeline, and implications.

### 4. Ozempic/GLP-1 Drugs: Bigger Than AI Right Now
- GLP-1 drugs currently generate more revenue than many AI companies.
- Implications for healthcare economics.

### 5. Societal Reframe Needed
- Shift from scarcity thinking to abundance planning
- AI boosts both scientific productivity and personal efficiency

### 6. The Combined Vision
How AI + robotics + bold visions (Musk-style moonshots) reshape economy, governance, and human potential in the next decade.

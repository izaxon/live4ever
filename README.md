# Human Longevity & Rejuvenation Research

**Can we slow or reverse aging? This research tracks the answer.**

---

## What's This About?

This project explores ways to extend human lifespan and, more importantly, **healthspan** — the years lived in good health. 

The focus is on practical approaches for people **60 years and older** who want to:
- Understand the latest longevity science
- Make informed decisions about supplements and treatments
- Plan for future therapies

---

## The Big Picture (As of April 2026)

Scientists have identified several promising approaches:

| Approach | Available Now? | Cost | Key Evidence |
|----------|----------------|------|--------------|
| NAD+ Boosters (NMN/NR) | ✅ Yes | $20-80/mo | 18% improvement in physical fitness |
| Senolytics | ✅ Yes | $20-40/mo | Clears zombie cells, reduces inflammation |
| Epigenetic Reprogramming | ❌ No (trials) | $10k+ | Could reverse cell age — first human trials started |
| Gene Therapy | ❌ No | TBD | Multiple approaches in development |

**Bottom line:** You can start some approaches TODAY. Others are 5-10 years away.

# Final Recommendation: Best Approaches for Elderly (60-80)

## Executive Summary

Based on comprehensive comparative analysis and timeline research, this recommendation provides evidence-based guidance for elderly adults (60-80) considering rejuvenation approaches.

**Primary Recommendation: For immediate cardiovascular benefit, nattokinase (10,800 FU/day). For systemic aging, consider NAD+ boosters (NR 300-500mg/day) with nattokinase combination.**

---

## 1. Best Approach by Goal

### If Primary Goal is Cardiovascular Health

| Rank | Approach | Evidence Strength | Cost/Mo | Recommendation |
|------|----------|------------------|---------|---------------|
| **#1** | **Nattokinase** (10,800 FU/day) | Strong (n=1,062, 22-36% plaque reduction) | $20-40 | ✅ Start now |
| #2 | Statins | Standard of care | $150-400 | Discuss with doctor |

**Rationale**: 
- Largest human trial showing direct plaque reduction
- Cost a fraction of statins
- No significant side effects in 12-month study
- Sinclair takes daily, monitors with ultrasound

### If Primary Goal is Systemic/Metabolic Health

| Rank | Approach | Evidence Strength | Cost/Mo | Recommendation |
|------|----------|------------------|---------|---------------|
| **#1** | **NR (Nicotinamide Riboside)** | Moderate (2x NAD+, improved walking) | $40-100 | ✅ Start now |
| #2 | NMN | Moderate (NAD+ elevation, walking speed) | $40-150 | Available internationally |

**Rationale**:
- Confirmed to raise NAD+ ~2-fold in humans
- Improved 6-min walk by 17.6m in PAD patients
- Available OTC (NR) or research compound (NMN)
- Excellent safety profile

### If Primary Goal is Addressing Cellular Senescence

| Rank | Approach | Evidence Strength | Cost/Mo | Recommendation |
|------|----------|------------------|---------|---------------|
| **#1** | **Monitor Only** | N/A | N/A | Await Phase 2/3 data |
| #2 | Fisetin (low dose) | Preliminary | $30-60 | Consider in trials |

**Rationale**:
- Senolytics efficacy tied to high senescence burden
- Evidence strongest in specific subpopulations
- Not yet FDA-approved
- Recommend awaiting larger trials (2027-2028 data)

### If Seeking Cutting-Edge/Long-Term

## 6. Key Takeaways

1. **Best immediate value**: Nattokinase - cheapest, strongest CV data, 66-95% improvement rates
2. **Best systemic support**: NAD+ boosters (NR) - available now, good safety
3. **Most transformative**: Epigenetic reprogramming - but 2029+ for availability
4. **For best results**: Combine nattokinase + NAD+ boosters (different mechanisms)
5. **Always**: Consult physician before starting (Sinclair's caveat)

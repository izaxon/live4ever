# Timeline Analysis: When Will Approved Therapies Be Available?

## Executive Summary

This analysis estimates timelines for FDA approval and widespread availability of the four rejuvenation approaches. Key finding: **Nattokinase is available now (OTC supplement), NAD+ boosters available OTC now (NR) or research compound (NMN). First FDA-approved "longevity" therapy likely 2028-2030**, with epigenetic reprogramming leading if Phase 1/2 data proves positive.

---

## Current Regulatory Status by Approach

### 1. Senolytics

| Status | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Regulatory path** | Drug repurposing (dasatinib FDA-approved for CML) |
| **Current trials** | Phase 2 (multiple ongoing) |
| **FDA approval** | Not expected before 2028-2030 |
| **Key trials** | TAME trial (n=3,000, target), CONFIRM-IPF, ALKASALT-AD |

**Timeline:**
- 2026: Multiple Phase 2 trials ongoing
- 2027-2028: Phase 2/3 readouts expected
- 2029-2030: Potential first FDA approval (disease-specific)
- **Realistic estimate: 2029-2032** for first indication

### 2. NAD+ Boosters

| Compound | Regulatory Status | Availability |
|----------|-----------------|-------------|
| **NR (Nicotinamide Riboside)** | GRAS, OTC supplement | Now |
| **NMN** | Removed from supplement pathway (Nov 2022), IND filed | Research compound / international |

**Timeline:**
- NR: Available as OTC supplement (no FDA approval needed)
- NMN: FDA treating as investigational new drug - could require IND for approval
- **NR available now; NMN pharmaceutical approval 2030+ if pursued**

### 3. Epigenetic Reprogramming

| Status | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Regulatory path** | Gene therapy (AAV-OSK) |
| **Current trials** | Phase 1 (NCT07290244) - started Q1 2026 |
| **Target** | Optic neuropathies (glaucoma, NAION) |
| **FDA approval** | Could be accelerated if Phase 1/2 successful |

**Life Biosciences ER-100 Timeline:**
- Q1 2026: First patient dosed
- 2026-2027: Phase 1 safety data
- 2027-2028: Phase 2 initiation (if Phase 1 successful)
- 2029: Potential NDA submission
- **First approval possible: 2029-2030** (eye disease)
- **Systemic applications: 2032+**

### 4. Nattokinase

| Status | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Regulatory path** | Dietary supplement |
| **Current status** | Available OTC |
| **FDA drug approval** | Not pursued (supplement status) |

**Timeline:**
- Already available as supplement
- Drug development not currently pursued
- If pursued as drug: 2030+ (requires Phase 3 trial)

## First FDA-Approved Therapy Prediction

### Most Likely First FDA "Longevity" Approval

| Rank | Approach | Timing | Reasoning |
|------|----------|--------|----------|
| 1 | **Epigenetic reprogramming** | 2029-2030 | Specific disease target (eye), strong preclinical, FDA clear path |
| 2 | **Senolytics (UBX1325)** | 2030+ | Local injection (eye), already Phase 2b |
| 3 | **NAD+ boosters** | 2032+ | Requires disease indication, not supplement |
| 4 | **Nattokinase** | Not pursued | Supplement path, no drug development |

### Why Reprogramming Leads

1. **Targeted approach**: Eye disease (not systemic) = clearer regulatory path
2. **Strong company**: Life Biosciences, substantial funding
3. **Validated mechanism**: OSK factors well-characterized
4. **First-in-class**: precedent for future longevity drugs

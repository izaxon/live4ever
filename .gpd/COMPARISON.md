# Comparative Analysis: Four Rejuvenation Approaches for Elderly (60-80)

## Executive Summary

This analysis compares four approaches to rejuvenation and health extension for elderly adults (60-80): senolytics, NAD+ boosters, epigenetic reprogramming, and nattokinase. Key finding: **each approach targets different mechanisms and populations**, with nattokinase being the most accessible and immediately available, while epigenetic reprogramming represents the most transformative but furthest from widespread use.

---

## 1. Mechanism of Action

### Senolytics
- **Target**: Senescent cells ("zombie cells") that accumulate with age and secrete inflammatory SASP factors
- **Mechanism**: Selective apoptosis induction in p16INK4a-positive senescent cells
- **Drugs**: Dasatinib + Quercetin (D+Q), Fisetin
- **Effect**: Removes damaged senescent cells, reduces systemic inflammation

### NAD+ Boosters
- **Target**: Declining NAD+ levels with age (declines ~50% by age 50)
- **Mechanism**: Precursor supplementation increases cellular NAD+ pools
- **Compounds**: Nicotinamide Mononucleotide (NMN), Nicotinamide Riboside (NR)
- **Effect**: Restores sirtuin/PARP activity, improves mitochondrial function

### Epigenetic Reprogramming
- **Target**: Epigenetic "noise" - DNA methylation patternsaccumulated with age
- **Mechanism**: OSK factors (Oct4, Sox2, Klf4) reset epigenetic age
- **Delivery**: AAV gene therapy (local injection)
- **Effect**: Partial reprogramming - reverses epigenetic age without full pluripotency

### Nattokinase
- **Target**: Fibrin/fibrinogen and arterial plaque
- **Mechanism**: Oral fibrinolytic enzyme (fermented soy derivative)
- **Dose**: 10,800 FU/day (effective dose)
- **Effect**: Dissolves arterial plaque, reduces CCA-IMT

## 8. Key Conclusions

1. **Immediate accessibility**: NAD+ boosters and nattokinase are available now as OTC supplements with good safety data

2. **Most transformative potential**: Epigenetic reprogramming (if systemic delivery proven safe) but farthest from widespread use

3. **Best CV benefit**: Nattokinase shows strongest cardiovascular outcomes with lowest cost

4. **Population-specific**: Senolytics show strongest effects in high-senescence populations (high p16 expression)

5. **Combination potential**: Approaches target different mechanisms - combinations may be synergistic
